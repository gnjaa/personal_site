About Me

Giselle Njaastad

I am a Front-End Developer based in the Chicago area with an AAS in Web Development and four years of experience in team management.

My current area of technical focus is web accessibility. I'm continually learning how to build interfaces that are more inclusive and usable for everyone. I believe accessibility is a key part of good design because it helps create engaging, responsive experiences for users of all abilities.

While I have my preferred front-end tools, I am actively expanding my skill set, whether that means jumping into a new CMS or learning a new development environment. My experience leading a team has shaped how I approach development: organized, communicative, and always looking for ways to improve.
